I do have a good body of coding experience. by now. I'm always looking to partner with researchers at academic instutions to write grants with you, as well as individuals in industry, but I don't think focusing a whole business on a mobile app is the way to go anymore. I think there is space for grants that include mobile applications, but from what I've seen they have to be about making a really positive difference in people's lives and not worrying about revenue. It's hard to pitch a purely recreational and social app when Facebook, SnapChat, and Instagram have had a 7 year headstart on whatever your idea is. If you want to go that route, it's a really crowded market but from experience NSF is at least willing to entertain these proposals.

Web and SEO is really interesting too. Finding a good domain for your company is arduous.
